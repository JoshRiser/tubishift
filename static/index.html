🔐 Tubi Authentication
Tubi requires two cookies from your browser. This is a one-time setup — they are saved locally on your computer.
1
Open Chrome or Firefox and go to tubitv.com. Log in or browse as guest.
2
Press F12 → Application tab (Chrome) or Storage tab (Firefox) → Cookies → https://tubitv.com.
3
Find the cookie named at, double-click its Value column to select the whole value, then paste it below.

🧩 Install TubiShift Extension
The Chrome extension enables auto-advance — when an episode ends on Tubi, it automatically loads the next one in your queue.
⬇ Download Extension (.zip)
1
Click the button above to download tubishift-extension.zip, then unzip it to a folder on your computer.
2
Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions. Enable Developer mode using the toggle in the top-right corner.
3
Click Load unpacked and select the unzipped tubishift-extension folder.
4
The 📺 TubiShift icon will appear in your toolbar. Launch your channel here, then open an episode on Tubi — it'll advance automatically.
